What it does

Two tools in one app. Both local. Both fast.

Local meeting recording

Captures mic and system audio as WAV. No bots joining your calls. No one knows you're recording.

Whisper transcription

Runs Whisper locally on your Mac. Pick your model size. No audio leaves your device. Ever.

Push-to-talk dictation

Hold ⌥ Space and talk. Clean text appears wherever your cursor is. Like magic, but on your machine.

AI summaries

Ollama for local, or OpenAI/Anthropic if you prefer. Key points, decisions, and action items extracted automatically.

Fully offline

Local Whisper + local LLM. Your voice never leaves your Mac. Not because we're noble, just because it's simpler.

Snippets & dictionary

Custom words, text snippets, and voice commands. Teach it your jargon so it stops mangling your CEO's name.

How it works

Brace yourself for the complexity.

1

Run onboarding

Grant permissions, pick a Whisper model, optionally set up Ollama. Takes about a minute if you don't get distracted.

2

Record or dictate

Hit record for meetings — minimize to a floating pill. Or hold ⌥ Space to dictate anywhere.

3

Text appears

Meetings get transcripts, summaries, and action items. Dictation drops clean text at your cursor. That's it. No step four.

Privacy

We don't want your data

There's no server. No analytics. No account. No telemetry. Frajola can run fully offline because we couldn't be bothered to build a backend.

  • On-device Whisper transcription
  • Apple Speech for real-time dictation
  • Works with Ollama & LM Studio
  • No account, no sign-up, no nothing
